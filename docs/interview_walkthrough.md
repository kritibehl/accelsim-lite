# AccelSim-Lite Interview Walkthrough

## 60 seconds
AccelSim-Lite turns performance regressions into named bottlenecks with actionable fixes.

## 3 minutes
It runs workload profiles, classifies stalls, compares p95 latency, and gates regressions.

## 10 minutes
Walk through compute-heavy vs memory-heavy bottlenecks, regression detection, sanitizer checks, and optimization recommendation.
